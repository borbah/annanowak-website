She teaches regularly at The Place and Trinity Laban in London and has tought in Academia Nazionale de Danca in Rome, Het Muzik Theater in Amsterdam, Beijing City Ballet, Ballet Moscow, Centre Choreographique in Montreal and many other prestigious institutions.

Anna has a great passion for education and sharing with others. She is interested in looking at ways in which the knowledge and experience can be delivered for the highest benefit of the student.

She appreciates the wide range of skills necessary for a Dance Artist to develop, from musicality and phrasing, through body awareness and understanding of biomechanics, to details and nuances, which make the dancer feel and look amazing.

She teaches Ballet technique, focusing on the alignment at the barre and then on more elaborate phrases at the centre, developing coordination and stimulating the nervous system to adjust to different dynamics, directions and transitions. She also teaches contemporary technique, based on the Cunningham system.

She loves giving dancers the opportunity to move in the space, to travel and explore the edges of balance and the tension between control and risk- taking. Anna also teaches various courses and workshops for dancers, focusing on body awareness and physical strength and flexibility, incorporating her understanding of Yoga (Anna is a certified Yoga Teacher, look at MoveOm) and other techniques.
