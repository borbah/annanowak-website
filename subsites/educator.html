Anna has a great passion for education and sharing with others. She is interested in looking at ways in which the knowledge and experience can be delivered for the highest benefit of the student. She appreciates the wide range of skills necessary for a Dance Artist to develop, from musicality and phrasing, through body awareness and understanding of biomechanics, to details and nuances, which make dancer feel and look amazing.

Anna has led various educational projects, teaching  & choreographing for both professionals and non-professionals of all ages and abilities. She has taught numerous Masterclasses and Workshops around the world, teaching both Classical and Contemporery technique classes as well as creating under the umbrella of the Company Wayne McGregor. She taught at The Place and Trinity Laban in London, Academia Nazionale de Danca in Rome, Het Muzik Theater in Amsterdam, Beijing City Ballet, Ballet Moscow, Centre Choreographique in Montreal and many other prestigious institutions in the world.

Anna teaches Ballet technique, focusing on the alignment at the barre and then on more elaborate phrases at the centre, developing coordination and stimulating the nervous system to adjust to different dynamics, directions and transitions. She also teaches contemporary technique, based on the Cunningham system. She loves giving dancers the opportunity to move in the space, to travel and explore the edges of balance and the tension between control and risk- taking.

Anna also teaches various courses and workshops for dancers, focusing on body awareness and physical strength and flexibility, incorporating her understanding of Yoga and other techniques she encountered during her own dancing career.
