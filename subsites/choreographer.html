choreography

After Anna’s fruitful career as a professional dancer her passion for creating took her back to the studio in order to choreograph. Magic happens during the creative process: something appears out of nothing, ideas crash and evolve, and problem solving at the highest level is demanded. Creation IS playing with form in the most tangible way – for Anna this is the space where choreography comes to life. Movement is everywhere, it has always been and always will be part of our lives and as such can serve as the ultimate form of expression. 

Sola Traler

Choreography by Anna Nowak

Anna has choreographed several works and she continues developing new projects and ideas. Her works have been presented at various festivals and galas. These include FLUX, commissioned by the Malta Festival in Poznan in 2013 and V3, shown at the Kings Place in London, “Raw Materials” performed in the Great Theatre in Lodz and commissioned by the Youth Spirit of Dance International Gala and others. Her choreography “SOLA” was awarded the first prize in the choreographic competition ‘The Essense of Motion” in 2017.
