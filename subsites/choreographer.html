Where is it coming from, this need to make something out of nothing? How is the process of creation forcing its way through our senses and receptors? How does our perception transmit information from the outside world into the inner world before emerging back into form?

During creative process Anna explores questions she feels presented with and manifests them on a physical plane when the need to dress up the indescribable becomes an imperative.

Finding what feels like the right expression of the right subject in the right moment in time is what makes her fascination with choreography alive and filled with curiosity. A certain kind of satisfaction is to be found when things come together into completeness, when creativity playing with form is allowed to make its own choices, finding its own aesthetic. Then agreeing with the form, which emerges from the process, becomes a simple act of connecting the unknown with obvious.  

Sola Traler

Choreography by Anna Nowak

Anna choreographed several works and she continues developing new projects and ideas. Her works were presented at various festivals and galas. These include FLUX, commissioned by the Malta Festival in Poznan in 2013 and V3, shown at the Kings Place in London, “Raw Materials” performed in the Great Theatre in Lodz and commissioned by the Youth Spirit of Dance International Gala and others. Her choreography “SOLA” was awarded the first prize in the choreographic competition ‘The Essense of Motion” in 2017.
